The Experience

Each day follows a relaxing rhythm designed to balance adventure with restoration.

You'll begin with an optional Morning Reset featuring gentle movement, breathwork, and meditation before heading into the African wilderness for a sunrise safari.

After a nourishing breakfast, you'll enjoy free time to relax, visit the sauna, schedule a massage or private Tibetan singing bowl session, paint, journal, photograph wildlife, or simply enjoy the peaceful surroundings.

Later in the day we'll gather for yoga or a Bush Wisdom session before heading back into the wild for another unforgettable safari. Evenings end with delicious dinners, yin yoga, meditation, sound healing, fireside gatherings, or guided stargazing beneath the African sky.

Two game drives or walks per day.

Safari is one of the highlights of this retreat. You'll experience multiple sunrise and afternoon game drives throughout the week, exploring different areas of the Greater Kruger wilderness during the times when wildlife is most active.

No two safaris are ever the same — and that's part of what makes them so unforgettable.

Greater Kruger is one of the world's premier wildlife destinations.

Depending on nature, you may encounter the Big Five — lion, leopard, elephant, rhinoceros, and buffalo — as well as giraffes, zebras, cheetahs, hippos, crocodiles, hyenas, wildebeest, kudu, impalas, warthogs, baboons, monkeys, and hundreds of bird species.

Every drive brings new surprises.

Our yoga sessions are designed for real life.
